## ML-002 - Make PCA n_components configurable + include in optimization search
**Status**: PASSED
**Date**: 2026-01-28
**RMSE_mean**: 2.930 (improved from 3.314)

### Changes Made:
- Added CLI arguments for n_components configuration:
  - --n_components (default: 50)
  - --n_components_min (default: 10)
  - --n_components_max (default: 100)
  - --optimize_n_components (flag to enable joint optimization)
- Added `optimize_with_pca()` function in src/optimization.py for joint optimization
- Wired CLI args to FeatureExtractor and optimization flow in main.py

### Learnings:
- Joint optimization of n_components + model hyperparameters yields better results than fixed n_components
- Optimal n_components for this dataset: 95 (vs default 50)
- TPESampler with 100 trials finds stable optimum around n_components=90-95 for Ridge
- Ridge alpha optimal range: ~4000-6000 when combined with higher n_components
- PLS model doesn't use PCA (has its own latent variable decomposition), handled as special case
